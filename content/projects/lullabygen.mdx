## Overview

LullabyGen is an AI assistant for generating custom lullabies and translating them across languages. The product combines emotional personalization with simple tooling for parents and educators.

## What I focused on

- Generate content tailored to a child or bedtime theme.
- Support multilingual families with smooth translation workflows.
- Keep the interface intuitive for non-technical users.

## Technical details

The app uses Streamlit for a fast feedback loop and OpenAI APIs for creative generation and translation. Prompt patterns are tuned to preserve tone while adapting vocabulary to age-appropriate and culturally flexible language.
