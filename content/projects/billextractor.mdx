## Overview

Bill Extractor automates structured data extraction from billing documents. It is designed for teams that need fast, reliable access to invoice fields without manual copy-and-paste workflows.

## What I focused on

- Converts unstructured bill content into usable fields.
- Reduces repetitive administrative effort.
- Enables downstream automation in finance and operations workflows.

## Technical details

The implementation uses AI-based parsing and prompt strategies to identify key entities from variable document formats. A Streamlit frontend keeps the process transparent and easy to validate with human review.
