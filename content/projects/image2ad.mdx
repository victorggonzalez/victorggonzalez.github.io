## Overview

Image2Ad transforms a simple product photo into a marketplace-ready ad draft. The app is designed for speed, helping users move from idea to publishable copy in seconds.

## What I focused on

- Reduces manual writing effort for repetitive ad creation.
- Improves consistency of listing quality and structure.
- Makes LLM workflows accessible through a lightweight UI.

## Technical details

The project combines Streamlit for rapid UX delivery and OpenAI models for text generation. Prompt design focuses on producing clear, conversion-oriented descriptions while preserving user intent from image and context input.
